Welcome to my web application, designed to help you track your daily workout routines and calorie intake. Upon signing up, you'll be prompted to enter your weight, age, and height, allowing the app to calculate your Metabolic Rate (MBR) for better health insights.

Should your personal details change, the app offers the flexibility to update your weight, age, height, and even your name, ensuring your BMR calculations remain accurate. For your convenience, there's also an option to #delete all data should you decide to start afresh.

Please note, I am not a certified dietitian or personal trainer. Therefore, it's important to interpret the information provided with caution. I cannot be held legally responsible for any health issues that may arise from using this application.

Enjoy using the app and here's to your health journey! :)
